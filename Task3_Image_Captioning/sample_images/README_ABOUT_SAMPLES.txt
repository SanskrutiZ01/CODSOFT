These are simple synthetically-generated placeholder images (basic shapes:
a red circle, a blue square, a green triangle) included so the project
runs and can be demoed out-of-the-box without requiring you to source
external photos.

For a more impressive GitHub/portfolio demo, replace these with a few
real-world photos (people, animals, landscapes, everyday scenes) -- BLIP
produces far more interesting and natural captions on real photographs
than on synthetic geometric shapes.
